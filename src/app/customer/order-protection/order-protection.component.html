Frequently Asked Questions

Order Protection is a service that helps protect your payments and ensures you receive the products as described. It includes payment security, dispute resolution, and quality assurance services depending on the protection level you choose.

Your payment is held securely by Alibaba until you confirm that you've received the order as described. If there are any issues, you can file a dispute within the protection period to get assistance in resolving the matter.

  • Basic Protection (Free): Standard payment protection and dispute resolution
  • Premium Protection ($9.99): Extended protection period, quality inspection, and priority service
  • Enterprise Protection ($29.99): Comprehensive protection with on-site inspection and legal support

You can upgrade your protection level within 7 days of placing the order. Downgrades are not permitted after order confirmation.
