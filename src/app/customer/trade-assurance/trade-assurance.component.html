Frequently Asked Questions

Trade Assurance is a free service offered by GlobalB2B that helps protect your payments when ordering from suppliers. It ensures that your payment is only released to the supplier after you confirm satisfactory receipt of your order.

Basic Trade Assurance protection is completely free. We also offer Premium Protection for 1.5% of your order value (minimum $15) and Enterprise Protection for 3% of your order value (minimum $50) with additional benefits.

Suppliers who offer Trade Assurance will display the Trade Assurance badge on their product pages and company profile. You can also filter search results to only show Trade Assurance suppliers.

Trade Assurance covers issues like non-delivery, late delivery, and products that don't match the description or quality specifications. Our team will help mediate disputes and may provide refunds or other resolutions when appropriate.
