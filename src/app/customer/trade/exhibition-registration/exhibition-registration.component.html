Exhibitor Terms and Conditions
1. Exhibitor Eligibility

To qualify as a GlobalB2B Exhibitor, your company must be a legally registered business with valid business licenses and the ability to conduct international trade.

2. Subscription Fees

Exhibitor packages are annual subscriptions. Fees are non-refundable after 30 days of activation. Automatic renewal will occur unless cancelled 30 days before renewal date.

3. Content Guidelines

All product listings and company information must be accurate and comply with our content policies. GlobalB2B reserves the right to remove any content that violates our guidelines.

4. Intellectual Property

Exhibitors guarantee they have all necessary rights to display and offer their products. GlobalB2B is not responsible for any IP infringement by exhibitors.

5. Verification Process

All exhibitors undergo verification which may include business license checks, factory audits, or product testing. Approval typically takes 3-5 business days.

6. Termination

GlobalB2B reserves the right to terminate any exhibitor account for policy violations, with no refund of remaining subscription fees.
