Trade Assurance Terms and Conditions
1. Overview

The GlobalB2B Trade Assurance program is designed to protect buyers and suppliers in international trade transactions. By participating in this program, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

2. Buyer Protection

Buyers are protected against:

  • Non-delivery of goods
  • Significant delay in delivery (beyond agreed timeframe)
  • Quality issues that don't match product specifications
3. Supplier Requirements

Suppliers must:

  • Provide accurate product descriptions
  • Meet agreed delivery timelines
  • Maintain quality standards as specified
  • Cooperate with dispute resolution processes
4. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes:

  • Both parties must attempt to resolve the issue directly first
  • GlobalB2B will mediate if resolution isn't reached within 7 days
  • Decisions by GlobalB2B mediators are binding for payment release
5. Fees and Payments

The Trade Assurance program is free for buyers. Suppliers pay a small service fee (1-3% of transaction value) only when they receive payment for a Trade Assurance order.
